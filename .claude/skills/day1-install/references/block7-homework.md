## EXPLAIN

### What to Practice Tonight

Before Day 2, complete these tasks to solidify what you learned:

### Task 1: Practice 3 Multi-turn Conversations
Pick 3 real work problems and solve them with Claude. Focus on:
- Giving clear context at the start
- Being specific about what you want
- Correcting course when Claude misunderstands
- Building on previous answers

**Ideas by role:**
| Role | Conversation idea |
|------|-------------------|
| HR | "Help me write interview questions for a [role] position" |
| Marketing | "Analyze our competitor's messaging and suggest improvements" |
| Sales | "Draft a follow-up email for a prospect who went quiet" |
| Finance | "Explain [financial concept] in simple terms for a team presentation" |
| Operations | "Create a process checklist for [recurring task]" |

### Task 2: Enhance Your CLAUDE.md
Add at least 2 more details to your CLAUDE.md:
- Your communication preferences (bullet points vs paragraphs, formal vs casual)
- Common tasks you'd like help with
- Tools or systems you work with daily
- Your team's key terminology or abbreviations

### Task 3: Web Search Report
Use Claude's web search to research a topic relevant to your team:
- Ask Claude to search for recent trends, news, or best practices
- Have Claude organize findings into a structured report
- Save the report as a file in your project folder

### Task 4: Try Plan Mode
Pick one complex, multi-step task and use Plan Mode:
- Type `/plan` or ask Claude to "plan this out first"
- Review Claude's plan before letting it execute
- Notice how the plan breaks big tasks into smaller steps

### Why This Matters for Day 2

Tomorrow you'll connect your work tools (Slack, Jira, Google Calendar, etc.) to Claude. The better you are at having clear conversations with Claude, the better your cross-tool queries will work.

Think of it this way:
- Today: Claude can read and write files, search the web, and help you think
- Tomorrow: Claude will also be able to read your Slack, check your calendar, look up Jira tickets, and more
